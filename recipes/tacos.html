Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Details:

Simple. Delicious. Amazing. No need to waste your time with other inferior taco recipes. This recipie will leave your guests drooling and BEGGING for more TACOS!

Just open flour OR corn tortillas and start stuffing! It's just that simple... and TASTY!

Add some vegetables, whatever floats your boat. Important: The key to remember here is that you put good stuff in the tacos! The tacos need cheese too. MORE cheese is certainly the appropriate amount of cheese to be using for these tacos.

Sauce on top... or in the middle... OR on bottom. HOT sauce is recommended, but the sauce does not NEED to be HOT if you are unable and/or unwilling for whatever reason to handle the HEAT!

Summer got ya' sizzlin'? DON'T heat-up the kitchen with your oven -- no need with these simple, gorgeous, spectacular tacos. They're so good as is, but you can cook them if you want to. Go ahead, figure it out.

Steps:

  1. Spread Tortillas Open
  2. Pack Tortillas with Vegetables
  3. Pack Tortillas with Cheese
  4. Top Tortillas with Sauce
  5. Fold Tacos, Bake to Taste
  6. Add Additional Toppings to Taste
