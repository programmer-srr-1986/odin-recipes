Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Details:

Steps:

  1. Spread Tortillas Open
  2. Pack Tortillas with Vegetables
  3. Pack Tortillas with Cheese
  4. Top Tortillas with Sauce
  5. Fold Tacos, Bake to Taste
  6. Add Additional Toppings to Taste
