Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables

Details:

This is the other recipie on this site. This one is not worse than the others though.

It's easy. Get some dough, preferably pre-made so you don't have to do as much work. You'll definitely want to put stuff on top of the dough before it goes in the oven. I know: more work. Maybe you should just order a pre-cooked pizza instead.

But you didn't order a precooked pizza instead. So now you're here. Congratulations!

Once you put all the stuff listed in the ingredients section in whatever amounts you wanted to, (or not,) I'm not judging you, maybe someone else is, but NOT me; you'll probably want to bake it. If you have a brick-oven, that would probably be nice. If you have one of those, you'll probably know what time/temperature to aim for when you cook the pizza.

If you don't have a brick-oven, a regular oven works too. I heard that 350 degrees farenheit for twenty minutes-or-so will probably be okay to at least start melting the cheese. GOOD LUCK!!

Steps:

  1. Prepare Dough
  2. Add Sauce
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Add Vegetables
  5. Cook Pizza
  6. Add Additional Toppings to Taste
