Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Details:

Literally any pasta will do. That's right: THIS recipe is so good that the pasta doesn't even matter. Just cook it until you can stick it to a wall and move-on with your life. After all: you have better things to do anyway -- like cooking meat, adding sauce, adding cheese, etcetera and so-on.

You're probably wondering, "But how much of each thing that's going into this will I even need?" Good news, GREAT news actually: Approximately one-fistful of each ingredient is exactly the PERFECT amount that is required to achieve greatness, realize your full potential AND be all that you can be. Cook the meat until it's brown... and you're good to go!

Red sauce? White sauce? Why not both?

When you're done addding whatever cheese you'd like, double it.

Add all of your prepared/pre-cooked ingredients to a cassarole dish, bake in a preheated oven at 400 degrees farenheit for twenty-five minutes, let sit for another ten minutes and your guests will love you. I guarantee it!

>

Steps:

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook Meat
  3. Add Sauce
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Add Meat
  6. Cook Cassarole
